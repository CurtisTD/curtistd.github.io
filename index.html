I am a graduate of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of South Florida. I am an avid software engineer, with a keen interest in algorithm design and computational geometry.

Outside of school, you can find me hiking, fishing, or practicing archery. I also spend a lot of time rebuilding and fixing cars.
